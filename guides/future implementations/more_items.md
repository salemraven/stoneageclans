### Current Resources in Stone Age Clans
From our past discussions, here's what we've established so far for gathering and crafting. These are pulled directly from approved mechanics like tree chopping, bush harvesting, building, knapping, and early food/hunting systems:

| Resource | Source | Primary Uses |
|----------|--------|--------------|
| **Wood/Branches/Logs** | Chopping trees (indefinite respawn nodes) | Building shelters/huts, fires, hafts/spears, fuel |
| **Berries** | Bushes (indefinite harvest) | Food, basic medicine |
| **Flint** | Surface rocks/outcrops | Knapping into choppers, axes, arrowheads (Oldowan tools onward) |
| **Herbs** | Wilderness foraging | Food, early medicine |
| **Stones** | Ground/rock piles | Building bases, hammerstones for knapping |
| **Hides** | Animal corpses (hunted/raided sheep/goats/wildlife) | Clothing, basic armor, building covers |
| **Bones** | Animal/human corpses (incl. cannibalism) | Tools, building reinforcement, weapons |

These support core loops: survival food, primitive tools via knapping minigame, and basic huts. Older characters get bonus yields via "Finesse" skill.

### Expanded Stone Age Resources
Drawing from prehistoric archaeology (flint knapping, hafting glues, animal parts), RimWorld primitive mods (bone/leather tech), and Stoneshard foraging (herbs/mushrooms), here's a variety of new collectibles. Focus on environmental nodes: respawning trees/bushes/rocks, finite animal drops, rare cave/ground spawns. Players mine/collect via chop/gather/hunt actions—indefinite for plants (e.g., tree bark/sap without felling), depletable for beasts/rocks till regen.

**Key Integration Ideas:**
- **Nodes Variety**: 20+ node types (e.g., "Gnarled Oak" for wood+bark+nuts; "Flint Vein" for knapping stones; "Dire Wolf Corpse" for multi-drops). Regen timers: plants 1-5 days, rocks/ores weekly.
- **Progression**: Early: surface gather. Mid: process into composites (e.g., sinew glue). Late: mine rares (copper nuggets beaten cold into jewelry for buffs like +strength or shaman rituals).
- **Risks**: Poison herbs, brittle stones shatter on knap fail, animal parts rot.
- **Crafting Chains**: Raw → Refined (simple process/fire) → Epic (multi-stage, rare catalysts).

### Grouped by Use & Crafting Chains
Grouped by **primary function**. Each includes **sources**, **refining path**, and **endgame epic** (2nd-stage for high-tier buffs/tech). Colors/textures fit pixel art: earthy browns/grays, blocky 64x64 sprites with frayed edges.

#### 1. **Food & Foraging** (Wild calories, indefinite bushes/trees)
| Resource | Sources/Nodes | Refining | Epic Products |
|----------|---------------|----------|---------------|
| Nuts/Acorns | Trees, ground | Roast → Meal | Pemmican (nuts+fat+berries: long storage) |

| Mushrooms | Damp caves/forests | Dry → Powder | Hallucinogen brew (visions for shaman buffs) |
| Honey | Beehives (trees/caves) | Raw jar | Mead (health regen, trade luxury) |
| Eggs/Fish/Shellfish | Nests, rivers, shores | Cook → Stew | Smoked (non-perish, bait for traps) |

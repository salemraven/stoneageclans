1. **Player-only Graves**  
   - Only the player character gets a grave when he dies.  
   - Must have lived to at least **Good** quality (age 46+) to qualify.  
   - Grave Mound building has **exactly 1 inventory slot** → holds **1 relic**.  
   - Once buried with a relic → permanent clan-wide buff (same strength as Shrine).  
   - Raiders can smash the grave → steal the relic + delete the buff (grave-robbing).

2. **Age-based Quality tiers for every male character** (player + clansmen)  
   - 13–25 → **Flawed** (grey border, –20 % stats)  
   - 26–45 → **Common** (white border, 0 %)  
   - 46–65 → **Good** (blue border, +15 %)  
   - 66–85 → **Fine** (light-blue border, +30 %)  
   - 86–100 → **Master** (purple border, +50 %)  
   - 90+ → **Legendary** (purple glow + aura, +60 %)

3. **Corpses & cannibalism**  
   - All dead NPCs (clansmen, women, player) drop a corpse.  
   - Corpse has colored border matching their final quality tier.  
   - You can either:  
     - Drag the corpse to Grave Mound (player only), or  
     - Chop it on the spot → yields meat + bone (explicit cannibalism, RP fuel).  
   - Large animals (mammoth, etc.) cannot be dragged – must be chopped on-site.

4. **Quality borders on EVERYTHING**  
   - All items, weapons, armor, buildings, NPCs, corpses → icon has a colored border showing its quality tier.  
   - Same color legend as above (grey = flawed, white = common, blue = good, light-blue = fine, purple = master/legendary).

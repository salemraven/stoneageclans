**Stone Age Clans** features a **gritty, hand-drawn 64×64 pixel-art style** for a top-down isometric survival game, evoking **RimWorld in the brutal Paleolithic era**. It's savage and grim, with **primitive cavemen, scruffy wild animals (like aurochs, boars, wolves), and ramshackle hide/wood/stone structures that look fragile and on the verge of collapse**—no polished villages, just desperate steppe camps.

### Core Visual Rules (The "Brand Bible")
- **Resolution & Readability**: 64×64 sprites (scale cleanly to 32×32 for UI/hotbar icons). **Strong silhouettes mandatory**—instantly readable at tiny sizes (e.g., caveman axe swing clear from afar). Top-down isometric (RimWorld angle: 30° tilt, consistent shadows bottom-right).
- **Palette**: **Strict 16-color muted earthy ramp** (browns/ochres #8B4513 to #D2B48C; dull greens #556B2F to #90EE90 desaturated; stony greys #696969 to #A9A9A9; muddy beiges #F5F5DC dirtied). **No bright/saturated colors, no pure black/white (except rare quality borders/highlights)**. Everything "dirty": subtle dirt specks, wear cracks, dry bloodstains on meat/tools.
- **Linework & Texture**: **Rough, scratched/uneven hand-drawn lines** (cave painting vibe—jagged, organic wobbles). Subtle **dirt/grime textures** (pixel noise, half-buried edges, moss patches). Low-res dithering for shading (no smooth gradients).
- **Tone & Theme**: **Grim/savage/exhausted**—tired muscular cavemen (fur loincloths, scars, no hair/beards on some for bald/wild look); scruffy beasts (matted fur, bony); temp shelters (torn hides flapping, crooked poles). **Pure prehistory: stone/wood/bone/hide/fire only—no metal, wheels, crops (wild only), fantasy/cute**. Moodles: cave glyph faces (frowning skulls, pained eyes).
- **Quality Tiers**: **Thin colored borders ONLY** (grey=normal, white=good, blue=superior, lt-blue=excellent, purple=masterwork, purple-glow=legendary). Base sprite unchanged.
